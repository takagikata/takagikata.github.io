# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 5, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **StellarWorks** ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in the **OrbitTimer** mobile application (the "App").

### 1. Information Collection and Use
We prioritize your privacy. The App is designed to function with minimal data collection.
- **Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers).
- **App Data:** Timer profiles, application settings, and timer states are stored locally on your device. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us.

### 2. Third-Party Services
The App utilizes third-party services that may collect data for specific purposes. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with these policies:

- **Google AdMob:** 
    - We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may use device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID or iOS IDFA) and cookies to serve ads based on your usage of this App and other apps or websites.
    - For more information, please visit: [How Google uses data](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).
    - You can manage your ad preferences or opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).
- **Google Fonts:**
    - The App uses Google Fonts to provide a consistent visual experience. Google may collect your IP address and other device information when loading fonts. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.

### 3. Device Features and Permissions
To provide its core functionality, the App requires access to certain device features:
- **Display and Power:** Managing screen brightness and preventing the device from entering sleep mode (Wakelock) during active timer sessions to ensure visibility.
- **Audio:** Playing sound effects to notify you of timer events, such as section transitions and timer completion.
- **Network Access:** Connecting to the internet to load advertisements and support essential app functions.
- **Local Storage:** Saving your customized profiles and settings to the device using local storage (Shared Preferences).

These permissions are used strictly to ensure the App functions as intended. We do not use these permissions to harvest personal information.

### 4. Children’s Privacy
The App is intended for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information from any user, we do not knowingly collect or maintain any data from children under the age of 13.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
