# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 24, 2024**

This Privacy Policy describes how **StellarWorks** ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in the **OrbitTimer** mobile application (the "App").

### 1. Information Collection and Use
We prioritize your privacy. The App is designed to function with minimal data collection.
- **Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers).
- **App Data:** Timer profiles, session history, and application settings are stored locally on your device using `shared_preferences`. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us.

### 2. Third-Party Services
The App utilizes third-party services that may collect data for specific purposes:
- **Google AdMob:** Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.
- **Google Fonts:** Used to provide a consistent visual experience.

### 3. Device Features and Permissions
To provide its core functionality, the App requires access to certain device features. These include, but are not limited to:
- **Display and Power:** Managing screen brightness and preventing the device from entering sleep mode (Wakelock) during active timer sessions.
- **Audio:** Playing alert and transition sounds to notify you of timer events.
- **Network Access:** Connecting to the internet to load advertisements and support essential app functions.
- **Local Storage:** Saving your customized profiles and settings to the device.

These permissions are used strictly to ensure the App functions as intended. We do not use these permissions to harvest personal information.
